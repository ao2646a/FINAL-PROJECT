College Choice Creator

Let us help you in finding the best fit for your undergraduate career. With around 5,300 different colleges in the U.S. to choose from, the school search process can seem daunting and be exhausting. Through filling out a couple of questions we can help narrow the search together.

Each page will have a stopwatch enabled on it. If you find yourself taking more than 5 minutes on each question, try to talk out the question with a friend or family member!

Let's get started!

College Choice Tool: Price

After 5 minutes, the survey will automatically move to the next question.


Colleges and universities range in price depending on if they are public, private, in-state, and out of state. By selecting your price range we can better select matches that will fit your needs. If in need of federal aid, feel free to check out the federal aid link here: FAFSA . There will be time to investigate federal aid options for each match at the end of the survey as well.

College Choice Tool: Size

After 5 minutes, the survey will automatically move to the next question.


Every college and university ranges in amount of students. Some schools have less than 3,000 students, whereas others have more than 60,000. While some prefer a large student body, others prefer a smaller one. Both styles offer opportunities for community building, therefore the decision is completely up to your preference. If you would prefer a compromise between these options, feel free to click medium.
